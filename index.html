ADMISSION

Admission will be done by the receptionist after registration at the admission counter. Categories of rooms such as private rooms of different types and general ward beds are available at the hospital with a different tariff.

During their stay in the Hospital patients will be billed on a daily basis and those bills have to be paid within two days.All Inpatient bills can be paid at the Inpatient Billing Counter.

Frequently Asked Questions

Health Care Questions !

  • WHOM TO CONTACT FOR HEALTH CHECK-UP AND IS PRIOR APPOINTMENT NECESSARY ?

    Please fix up prior appointment at our MHC department at the following number: +91 95007 12888, (0422) 4323727 between 9 am and 5 pm on all working days except Sundays and selected public holidays.

  • DOES THE PERSON REQUIRE TO FAST AND WHAT OTHER INSTRUCTION HE/SHE HAS TO FOLLOW ?

    Please take the following measures before you come for the health check-up :
    Before coming for health check-up, do not take anything but plain water if needed (not even coffee or tea) in the morning. A small stool sample should be brought in a clean plastic or glass container. If desired, the container may be collected from MHC dept. adjacent to main reception - Room No. 1004.
    Come on an empty stomach around 7.15 am.
    Do bring your previous medical records and the latest prescriptions / medicines.
    Complete your check-up by 6.00 pm (or earlier in certain cases) and collect your Master Health Check-up File.

  • CAN ADDITIONAL TESTS TO BE DONE ALONG WITH MASTER HEALTH CHECK-UP PROGRAM?

    Yes. Additional test can be done and would be charged extra. Certain tests are done at a concessional rate for Master Health Check-up patients.

  • WHO SHOULD UNDERGO HEALTH CHECK-UP ?

    Persons who fall under the following categories

    1. Age over 30 yrs
    2. Over weight / Obese
    3. Stressful & high tension job
    4. Smokers / Alcoholics
    5. High blood pressure
    6. Family history of diabetes or having diabetes
    7. High blood cholesterol
    8. Sendentary life style(not very active life style)
    9. Family history of heart disease.

  • WHETHER LUNCH WILL BE PROVIDED ?

    Yes, complimentary morning coffee / tea with biscuits will be served and lunch will be provided at the cafeteria.
